## 1. Layout Patterns

### Pattern A: Photo Collage Overlay (china_town.png)
- **Background:** Destination street-level photo with warm color wash
- **Foreground:** Cutout food/activity photos scattered across the lower half — irregular placement, slight rotation, no uniform grid
- **Text:** Large bold headline centered-top, smaller category labels next to each cutout
- **Decorative:** Themed sticker elements (lanterns, torii gate) placed around the edges
- **Best for:** Food tours, street food guides, market spotlights, "top picks" posts

### Pattern B: Full-Bleed Landscape (japan.png)
- **Background:** Single hero landscape photo filling the entire frame
- **Text:** Centered title stack — small subtitle above, large script/display headline, secondary info below
- **Decorative:** Minimal — let the photo do the work. Thin text line at the top for location list
- **Best for:** Trip announcements, destination reveals, vlog/blog covers, aspirational Reels covers

### Pattern C: Beach/Nature Highlight (Untitled design.png)
- **Background:** Vibrant destination photo (beach, nature, scenery)
- **Text:** Large bold brush-style headline overlaid directly on the photo, slightly off-center
- **Decorative:** Hand-drawn white doodles — speech bubbles, arrows, accent marks, small icons (clouds, waves, sparkles)
- **Best for:** Island/beach spotlights, quick tips, "you need to see this" discovery posts, story covers

## 2. Typography

### Headline Fonts
| Style | Usage | Seen In |
|---|---|---|
| **Bold brush / hand-painted** (Thai) | Primary headline — destination name, main hook | china_town.png, Untitled design.png |
| **Script / handwritten** (English) | Display headline for English-language posts | japan.png ("Japan" in script) |
| **Clean sans-serif** (bilingual) | Supporting city/location names, subtitles | japan.png top line |

### Text Hierarchy
1. **Primary headline** — largest element, 40-60% of frame width. Bold, high contrast against the photo. One or two words max.
2. **Subtitle / hook line** — smaller, positioned above or below the headline. Sets context (e.g., "A Trip to", "Episode 1").
3. **Category labels** — small text near individual cutout images or sections. Casual, descriptive (e.g., food names, location tags).
4. **Location tags** — thin, spaced-out text listing cities or stops. Often at the very top or bottom edge.

### Text Color Rules
- **White** on dark or blue-toned photos (japan.png)
- **Warm orange / golden yellow** (`~#E8891C` to `#F5A623`) on warm-toned or busy backgrounds (china_town.png, Untitled design.png)
- **Dark outlines or drop shadows** when placing bold text on varied backgrounds for legibility
- Never place light text on light areas without a shadow, stroke, or overlay

## 3. Color Patterns

### Warm Palette (Food & Street)
| Element | Color | Usage |
|---|---|---|
| Headline text | Warm orange `#E8891C` to `#D4641A` | Bold brush headlines |
| Accent text | Deep red-orange `#C0392B` | Secondary labels |
| Background wash | Warm golden overlay on photo | Unifies busy street scenes |

### Cool Palette (Landscape & Travel)
| Element | Color | Usage |
|---|---|---|
| Headline text | White `#FFFFFF` | Clean text on landscape photos |
| Subtitle text | White with slight transparency | Supporting lines |
| Background | Natural photo tones (blue sky, green hills) | No color overlay — photo speaks |

### Vibrant Palette (Beach & Nature)
| Element | Color | Usage |
|---|---|---|
| Headline text | Bold yellow `#F5C518` to `#E8A817` | Brush-style headlines |
| Doodle elements | White `#FFFFFF` | Hand-drawn accents |
| Background | Bright natural blues + greens from photo | No heavy overlay |

## 4. Decorative Elements

### Cutout Photos
- **Style:** Irregular edges, slight white border or shadow, no rigid rectangles
- **Placement:** Overlapping, scattered — looks like photos tossed on a table
- **Rotation:** Slight tilt (5-15 degrees) for energy
- **When to use:** Food roundups, activity highlights, multi-item showcases

### Hand-Drawn Doodles
- **Style:** White line art — speech bubbles, arrows, sparkles, clouds, waves, underlines
- **Weight:** Thin to medium stroke, casual feel
- **Placement:** Around headlines and key areas — frame the text, guide the eye
- **When to use:** Casual/fun posts — beach, discovery, tips, stories

### Themed Stickers
- **Style:** Small illustrated icons matching the destination theme (lanterns for Chinatown, torii gate for Japan, etc.)
- **Placement:** Corners and edges — decorative framing, not center stage
- **When to use:** Destination-specific posts where cultural icons add context

### Minimal / None
- **When to use:** Aspirational landscape shots, trip announcements — let the photo breathe

## 5. Photo Treatment

| Treatment | When | Example |
|---|---|---|
| **Warm color wash** | Street scenes, food, markets — busy backgrounds that need visual unity | china_town.png |
| **No filter / natural** | Hero landscapes, beaches — the natural color is the selling point | japan.png, Untitled design.png |
| **Slight brightness boost** | Beach/water shots — makes blues pop and water look inviting | Untitled design.png |

### Photo Selection Rules
- Lead with the destination's most visually distinct element (Mt. Fuji, turquoise water, neon street signs)
- Avoid overly staged or stock-looking photos — candid and immersive beats polished
- For cutout overlays, use photos with clean edges (food on plates, distinct objects) that separate easily from backgrounds

## 6. Format & Sizing

| Platform | Aspect Ratio | Resolution |
|---|---|---|
| Instagram Feed (square) | 1:1 | 1080 x 1080 px |
| Instagram Feed (portrait) | 4:5 | 1080 x 1350 px |
| Instagram / TikTok Reels cover | 9:16 | 1080 x 1920 px |
| Facebook post | 1.91:1 | 1200 x 628 px |
| YouTube thumbnail | 16:9 | 1280 x 720 px |

Current templates appear to be approximately **16:9** format — suitable for YouTube thumbnails, Facebook covers, and blog headers.
