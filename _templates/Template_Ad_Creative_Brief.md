## For Meta Ads (Instagram / Facebook)

### Ad Format
[Single image / Carousel / Video / Stories]

### Visual
- **Description:** [What does the image or video show?]
- **Style:** [Photo / Graphic / UGC-style / Testimonial card]
- **Specs:** 1080x1080 (feed) / 1080x1920 (Stories) / 1080x1080 (carousel cards)

### Primary Text (above the image)
[Main ad copy — 125 characters visible before "See more", full text up to 3-4 sentences]

**Line 1 (hook):**
[Destination + emotion, curiosity, or benefit]

**Lines 2-3 (body):**
[Key selling points — small groups, local guides, all-inclusive, social proof]

**Line 4 (CTA):**
[Price anchor + action — "From $1,649 all-inclusive. Book your spot →"]

### Headline (below the image, bold)
[Under 40 characters. Benefit-driven or destination + trip type]
Example: "Vietnam in 12 Days — All-Inclusive"

### Description (below the headline, gray text)
[Under 30 characters. Supporting info.]
Example: "Small groups. Local guides."

### CTA Button
[Book Now / Learn More / Sign Up / Get Offer]

## Checklist

- [ ] Ad copy matches GoTravel voice
- [ ] Value proposition is clear within 3 seconds
- [ ] Social proof included (rating, reviews, number of trips)
- [ ] Price or price anchor included (if conversion-focused)
- [ ] No policy violations (check Meta/Google ad policies)
- [ ] UTM parameters planned for tracking
- [ ] Landing page URL confirmed and working
