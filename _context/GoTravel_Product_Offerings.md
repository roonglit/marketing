# GoTravel — Product Offerings

## Trip Categories

### 1. Signature Trips
Our flagship multi-country or single-country itineraries. Fully designed, all-inclusive, departing on set dates.

### 2. Express Trips
Shorter 3-5 day trips focused on one region or city. Great for travelers with limited time or as add-ons to Signature Trips.

### 3. Private Trips
Any Signature or Express trip can be booked privately for groups of 4+. Same itinerary, exclusive group, flexible dates.

## Pricing & Booking Details

### What's Always Included
- Accommodation (boutique 3-star hotels, local guesthouses, 1-2 unique stays per trip)
- All in-country transport (buses, boats, domestic flights where listed)
- English-speaking local guides for the full trip
- Breakfast daily + selected lunches/dinners as listed
- All entrance fees and activity costs
- Airport pickup on Day 1

### What's Never Included
- International flights to/from home country
- Travel insurance (required — we recommend World Nomads or SafetyWing)
- Visa fees (we provide guidance for each country)
- Personal expenses (drinks, souvenirs, tips)
- Optional add-on activities (marked as optional in itineraries)

### Booking Policy
- **Deposit:** $300 to secure your spot (applied to total price)
- **Full payment:** Due 45 days before departure
- **Cancellation:** Full refund (minus $50 admin fee) if cancelled 30+ days out; 50% refund 15-29 days; no refund under 15 days
- **Date changes:** Free date change up to 30 days before departure (one change per booking)
- **Minimum travelers:** Trip runs with minimum 4 bookings; if under 4, we offer a date change or full refund

### Payment Methods
- Credit/debit card (Visa, Mastercard, Amex)
- PayPal
- Bank transfer (for private group bookings)
- Afterpay / Klarna available for US, UK, and AU customers (split into 4 payments)
