# GoTravel — Growth Marketing Context

## Current Marketing Goals (Q2 2026)

### Primary Goal
Increase direct bookings by 40% compared to Q2 2025 (from 85 to 120 bookings/month) by scaling paid acquisition and improving conversion rates on trip pages.

### Secondary Goals
- Grow email list from 8,500 to 15,000 subscribers by end of Q2
- Increase Instagram following from 12K to 25K
- Launch YouTube channel and publish 12 videos by end of June
- Achieve 30% email open rate (currently 24%)
- Reduce cost per acquisition on Meta Ads from $45 to $30

## Marketing Funnel & Metrics

| Stage | Channel | Current Metric | Target |

## Channel Strategy

### SEO & Blog (Organic — Top of Funnel)
- **Goal:** Drive organic traffic from travelers researching Southeast Asia trips
- **Content types:** Destination guides, itinerary breakdowns, "best time to visit" articles, packing lists
- **Target keywords:**
  - "best Vietnam itinerary 2 weeks" (2,400 searches/mo)
  - "Thailand small group tours" (1,800 searches/mo)
  - "Southeast Asia travel guide" (4,100 searches/mo)
  - "Chiang Mai day trips" (1,300 searches/mo)
  - "Cambodia vs Vietnam" (900 searches/mo)
- **Publishing cadence:** 2 blog posts per week (Tuesday and Thursday)
- **SEO tools:** Ahrefs, Google Search Console, SurferSEO

### Social Media

#### Instagram (@gotraveladventures)
- **Followers:** 12,000
- **Posting schedule:** 5x/week (3 feed posts + 2 Reels)
- **Content mix:** 40% trip highlights, 25% traveler stories/UGC, 20% destination education, 15% behind-the-scenes/team
- **Best performing content:** Reels with "day in the life on our Vietnam trip" format — avg 15K views
- **Engagement rate:** 4.2% (industry avg is 1.5%)

#### YouTube (launching Q2 2026)
- **Goal:** 1,000 subscribers in first 3 months
- **Content types:** Full trip vlogs (10-15 min), destination guides (5-8 min), "is it worth it?" reviews
- **Publishing cadence:** 1 video/week (Wednesdays)
- **Thumbnail style:** Destination photo + face + bold 3-word title

#### TikTok (@gotraveladventures)
- **Followers:** 3,200
- **Content:** Short-form Reels repurposed + trending audio content
- **Posting schedule:** 4x/week
- **Goal:** Reach 10K followers by end of Q2

#### Facebook
- **Primarily used for:** Ads, community group (GoTravel Explorers — 2,100 members), event promotion
- **Organic posting:** 3x/week, mostly repurposed from Instagram

### Email Marketing
- **Platform:** ConvertKit
- **List size:** 8,500 subscribers
- **Segments:**
  - New subscribers (< 30 days)
  - Past travelers
  - Abandoned checkout
  - Country-specific interest (Vietnam, Thailand, etc.)
- **Active sequences:**
  - Welcome series (5 emails over 10 days) — 42% open rate
  - Abandoned checkout (3 emails over 5 days) — 8% recovery rate
  - Post-trip follow-up (3 emails) — drives reviews and referrals
- **Newsletter:** Weekly "Travel Dispatch" every Saturday — destination stories, new trips, travel tips
- **Lead magnets:**
  - "Ultimate Southeast Asia Packing List" (PDF) — 4.1% conversion rate
  - "7-Day Vietnam Itinerary" (PDF) — 5.8% conversion rate
  - "SE Asia Budget Calculator" (interactive) — 3.5% conversion rate

### Paid Advertising
- **Total monthly budget:** $4,500
- **Meta Ads (Instagram + Facebook):** $3,000/month
  - Campaign types: Prospecting (Lookalike audiences), Retargeting (website visitors, email list), Trip launch promos
  - Best performing ad: Carousel of trip photos + traveler testimonial — $28 CPA
  - Worst performing: Static image with discount code — $62 CPA
- **Google Ads:** $1,200/month
  - Search campaigns targeting high-intent keywords: "book Vietnam tour", "Southeast Asia group tour"
  - Average CPC: $1.80
  - Conversion rate: 3.4%
- **TikTok Ads:** $300/month (testing phase)
  - Spark Ads boosting top-performing organic content
  - Early results: strong reach but low conversion — better for awareness

### Referral & Partnerships
- **Referral program:** Past travelers get $100 credit for each friend who books; friend gets $50 off
- **Travel blogger partnerships:** 3 active partnerships — free trips in exchange for content + affiliate links (8% commission)
- **Affiliate program:** 10% commission for travel bloggers and content creators, tracked via Impact.com

## Competitor Landscape (Marketing-Specific)

| Competitor | Strengths | Weaknesses | Our Advantage |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Intrepid Travel** | Massive brand, huge SEO presence, strong sustainability story | Trips feel corporate, groups of 15-20 | Smaller groups, more personal, local-led |
| **G Adventures** | Wide range of destinations, strong community | Similar "mass small-group" feel, mixed reviews on guide quality | Higher guide quality, more curated itineraries |
| **GetYourGuide** | Huge marketplace, strong Google Ads | Day tours only, no multi-day trips, no brand loyalty | Multi-day immersive experiences, relationship-building |
| **Local operators** | Cheap pricing, local knowledge | No online presence, inconsistent quality, no reviews | Professional booking experience, vetted quality, English support |

## Current Challenges

1. **Seasonality:** Bookings drop 50% in May-August (rainy season) — need off-season campaigns
2. **CAC rising:** Meta Ads costs up 20% year-over-year — need to diversify acquisition
3. **Content bottleneck:** Only 1 person creating content — need to scale with UGC and templates
4. **Low YouTube presence:** Competitors dominate video search — need to catch up fast
5. **Email list quality:** 30% of list is inactive (no opens in 6+ months) — need re-engagement or cleanup
