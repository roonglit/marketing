# GoTravel — Brand Context

## Company Overview

**Company Name:** GoTravel Adventures
**Founded:** 2019
**Headquarters:** Chiang Mai, Thailand
**Website:** www.gotravel-adventures.com

GoTravel Adventures is a tour operator specializing in curated small-group travel experiences across Southeast Asia. We design immersive itineraries that go beyond typical tourist routes — connecting travelers with local communities, hidden landscapes, and authentic cultural experiences.

## Mission

To make meaningful travel accessible to everyone — not just seasoned backpackers — by removing the stress of planning and replacing it with expertly crafted adventures that create lasting memories.

## Vision

Become the most trusted name in Southeast Asian small-group travel by 2028, known for responsible tourism, exceptional local guides, and trips that change how people see the world.

## Brand Story

GoTravel was started by Nate and Ploy, a couple who spent 3 years living and traveling across Southeast Asia. They kept meeting travelers who wanted authentic experiences but didn't know where to start — or ended up on overcrowded, cookie-cutter tours that felt more like cattle herding than travel. They founded GoTravel to bridge that gap: real adventures, small groups, fair prices, and a genuine connection to the places visited.

## Core Values

1. **Authenticity over spectacle** — We take people to real places, not tourist traps
2. **Small groups, big experiences** — Max 12 travelers per trip for a personal feel
3. **Travel as a force for good** — We pay local guides fairly, support community projects, and minimize environmental impact
4. **Accessible adventure** — You don't need to be a hardcore backpacker to have the trip of a lifetime
5. **No hidden costs** — Our pricing is transparent; what you see is what you pay

## Key Differentiators

- **Small group guarantee:** Max 12 people per trip (industry average is 30-40)
- **Local-led:** Every trip is led by guides who are from the region, not expat tour leaders
- **All-inclusive pricing:** Accommodation, transport, meals, activities — no surprise fees
- **Responsible tourism certified:** B Corp pending, 1% of revenue goes to local community projects
- **Flexible booking:** Free cancellation up to 30 days, easy date changes

## Company Facts

- Operated 280+ trips since 2019
- 4.9/5 average rating across 1,200+ reviews on TripAdvisor and Google
- 6 countries covered: Thailand, Vietnam, Cambodia, Laos, Indonesia, Philippines
- 35% of customers are repeat travelers or referrals
- Team of 8 full-time staff + 40 local guides across the region
- Average trip price: $1,200-2,500 per person (7-14 days)

## Target Market Summary

- Age 25-45, primarily from US/Canada, UK, Australia, and Europe
- Mix of solo travelers, couples, and small friend groups
- Value experiences over luxury — willing to spend for quality but not looking for 5-star resorts
- Digitally savvy — discover and research travel on Instagram, YouTube, blogs, and Reddit
- Many are taking their first trip to Southeast Asia
