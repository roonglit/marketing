## Email 4: Objection Handling — Common Concerns
**Timing:** Day 7
**Subject:** Honest answers to the 5 questions everyone asks
**Preview text:** Is it safe? Is it worth the money? Can I go solo?

---

Hey {first_name},

Before people book a trip with us, they usually have the same 5 questions. So here they are — no fluff, no sales pitch, just honest answers.

**1. Is Southeast Asia safe?**
Yes. Vietnam, Thailand, Cambodia, Laos, Indonesia, and the Philippines are all very safe for travelers. Petty theft can happen in cities (like anywhere), but violent crime against tourists is extremely rare. Plus, you're with a local guide who knows the area — that's an added layer of safety you don't get traveling solo.

**2. Can I go alone?**
Absolutely. About 45% of our travelers come solo. By day 2, the group feels like friends. By day 5, you're sharing inside jokes. We hear "I went solo and left with 11 new friends" more than any other feedback.

**3. Is it worth the price?**
Our trips include accommodation, all in-country transport (including domestic flights), meals, activities, entrance fees, and local guides. When travelers price out the same itinerary independently, they typically spend the same amount or more — without the guide, without the group, and with all the planning stress.

**4. What if I need to cancel?**
Full refund (minus $50 admin fee) if you cancel 30+ days before departure. Free date changes up to 30 days out. We know plans change — we don't penalize you for it.

**5. Do I need to be fit?**
No. Our trips involve walking and some light hiking, but nothing extreme. If you can walk for 2-3 hours on uneven ground, you'll be fine. We always flag the more physical days (like the Sapa trek) so you know what to expect.

Still have a question I didn't cover? Hit reply — I'm happy to help.

Nate
