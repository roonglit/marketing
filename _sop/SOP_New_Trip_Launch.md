# SOP: New Trip Launch

## Purpose
Coordinate the full marketing launch when a new trip is added to the GoTravel lineup — from trip page creation through to paid promotion.

## Timeline
Start 6 weeks before the first available departure date.

## Tools Required
- Shopify (trip page)
- Canva Pro (visuals)
- ConvertKit (email)
- Meta Business Suite (ads)
- Later (social scheduling)
- Notion (project tracking)

---

## Phase 1: Pre-Launch (6-4 Weeks Before)

### Step 1: Gather Trip Assets
Collect from the operations team:
- [ ] Final itinerary (day-by-day breakdown)
- [ ] Pricing and what's included/excluded
- [ ] Departure dates
- [ ] High-quality photos (minimum 15 — mix of landscapes, activities, food, people)
- [ ] 1-2 short video clips if available
- [ ] Guide bio and photo
- [ ] Any unique selling points or stories behind this trip

### Step 2: Write Trip Page Copy
Reference:
- `_context/GoTravel_Voice_Guide.md` for tone
- `_context/GoTravel_Product_Offerings.md` for format consistency

**Trip page structure:**
1. **Hero section:** Stunning photo + trip name + one-line hook + price + CTA
2. **Overview:** 2-3 paragraph description — paint a picture, don't just list facts
3. **Trip highlights:** 5-7 bullet points of the most exciting moments
4. **Day-by-day itinerary:** Collapsible accordion, each day with a title, description, and photo
5. **What's included / not included:** Clear two-column list
6. **Your guide:** Photo, name, bio, personal connection to the region
7. **Practical info:** Fitness level, best time to go, visa info, packing tips
8. **Pricing & booking:** Price, deposit info, cancellation policy, available dates with a booking button
9. **FAQ:** 5-7 common questions
10. **Social proof:** Reviews from beta testers or similar trips + overall GoTravel rating
11. **Related trips:** Links to 2-3 similar trips

### Step 3: Create Visual Assets
In Canva Pro, create:
- [ ] Trip page hero image (1920x1080)
- [ ] Social announcement graphic (1080x1080 for feed, 1080x1920 for Stories)
- [ ] Email header banner (600x300)
- [ ] Ad creatives — 3 variations:
  - Carousel (5 images showing the journey)
  - Single image with text overlay
  - Short video/slideshow (15 seconds for Reels/Stories)
- [ ] YouTube thumbnail if video content planned (1280x720)

### Step 4: Write Email Content
Draft 3 emails:

**Email 1 — Announcement (sent on launch day):**
- Subject line: Curiosity or destination-driven (e.g., "New trip just dropped: 12 days across [Country]")
- Body: What makes this trip special, 3-4 highlights, photos, link to trip page
- CTA: "Explore the full itinerary"

**Email 2 — Deep dive (sent 3 days after announcement):**
- Subject line: Focus on a specific highlight (e.g., "Imagine waking up to this view")
- Body: Story-driven — take the reader through one amazing day on the trip
- CTA: "Check available dates"

**Email 3 — Social proof + urgency (sent 7 days after announcement):**
- Subject line: Scarcity-driven (e.g., "Only X spots left on the first departure")
- Body: Early booking numbers, testimonial from beta trip or similar trip, booking deadline
- CTA: "Book your spot"

### Step 5: Prepare Social Content
Plan 2 weeks of social content around the launch:

| Day | Platform | Content Type | Topic |
|---|---|---|---|
| Launch Day | Instagram | Carousel + Reel | Trip announcement — highlights reel |
| Launch Day | Facebook | Post + Group post | Announcement with link to trip page |
| Day 2 | Instagram Stories | Poll/Quiz | "Guess the country" teaser using trip photos |
| Day 3 | Instagram | Single image | Hero photo + quote about the destination |
| Day 5 | TikTok + Reels | Short video | "POV: Day 1 of our new [Country] trip" |
| Day 7 | Instagram | Carousel | Day-by-day itinerary breakdown (visual) |
| Day 10 | Instagram Stories | Countdown sticker | "First departure in X weeks" |
| Day 14 | Instagram + Facebook | UGC/testimonial | Review or quote from beta traveler |

## Phase 2: Launch Day (Target: 4 Weeks Before First Departure)

### Step 6: Publish Trip Page
1. Publish the trip page on Shopify
2. Test the booking flow end-to-end (select dates → add to cart → checkout)
3. Test on mobile
4. Check all images load, links work, pricing is correct
5. Add the trip to the main navigation / "All Trips" page

### Step 7: Send Announcement Email
1. Send Email 1 to the full subscriber list (or segmented by country interest)
2. Monitor open rate and clicks in real-time for the first 2 hours
3. Fix any broken links immediately

### Step 8: Post Social Announcement
1. Publish the planned launch day content on all platforms
2. Post in GoTravel Explorers Facebook group
3. Engage with all comments and DMs within 2 hours

### Step 9: Notify Partners
1. Email affiliate partners with trip details and affiliate link
2. Reach out to travel blogger partners if relevant to their audience
3. Update the affiliate portal with new trip assets

---

## Phase 3: Post-Launch Promotion (Weeks 1-4 After Launch)

### Step 10: Launch Paid Ads
**Meta Ads:**
1. Create a Prospecting campaign:
   - Audience: Lookalike of past bookers + interest targeting (travel, Southeast Asia, adventure)
   - Creative: Test 3 ad variations (carousel, single image, video)
   - Budget: $30/day for first 2 weeks
   - Objective: Conversions (trip page view or booking)
2. Create a Retargeting campaign:
   - Audience: Website visitors (last 30 days) + email list
   - Creative: Social proof ad (testimonial + trip photos)
   - Budget: $15/day
3. Monitor daily for first 5 days, then every 2-3 days
4. Kill underperforming ads (CPA > $50) after 500+ impressions
5. Scale winners by increasing budget 20% every 3 days

**Google Ads:**
1. Add trip-specific keywords to search campaigns
   - e.g., "book [country] tour", "[country] small group travel"
2. Create a dedicated ad group with trip-specific headlines and descriptions
3. Budget: $20/day

### Step 11: Continue Email Sequence
- Day 3: Send Email 2 (deep dive)
- Day 7: Send Email 3 (social proof + urgency)
- Ongoing: Feature the new trip in the weekly Travel Dispatch newsletter for 4 weeks

### Step 12: Continue Social Cadence
- Follow the 2-week social content plan from Step 5
- After 2 weeks, integrate the trip into the regular content calendar (1-2 posts/week)
- Encourage early bookers to share on social and tag @gotraveladventures

### Step 13: Track Launch Performance
At the end of Week 2, compile a launch check-in:

| Metric | Target | Actual |
|---|---|---|
| Trip page views | 2,000 | |
| Bookings | 15 | |
| Email announcement open rate | 30% | |
| Ad CPA | < $35 | |
| Social reach (total) | 50,000 | |

At the end of Week 4, full launch report following the Campaign Performance Reporting SOP.

---

## Phase 4: Ongoing (After Launch Month)

### Step 14: Optimize & Iterate
1. Update trip page based on FAQ patterns (if people keep asking the same question, add it)
2. Add real traveler photos and reviews as they come in
3. A/B test ad creatives monthly
4. Write a blog post targeting SEO keywords for the destination (follow Blog Post Creation SOP)
5. Create a YouTube video for the trip if footage is available
6. Add trip to relevant comparison/roundup blog posts
