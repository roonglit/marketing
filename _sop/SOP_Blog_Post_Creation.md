# SOP: Blog Post Creation

## Purpose
Produce SEO-optimized, on-brand blog posts that drive organic traffic and move readers toward booking a trip.

## Frequency
2 posts per week (published Tuesday and Thursday)

## Tools Required
- Ahrefs (keyword research)
- SurferSEO (content optimization)
- Google Docs or Markdown editor (drafting)
- Canva Pro (featured image)
- Shopify CMS (publishing)

---

## Step 1: Topic Selection & Keyword Research

1. Check the content calendar in Notion for the next scheduled topic
2. If no topic assigned, choose based on:
   - Seasonal relevance (e.g., "best time to visit Vietnam" before peak booking season)
   - Keyword opportunity (high volume + low competition in Ahrefs)
   - Funnel stage needed (do we need more awareness or consideration content?)
3. In Ahrefs → Keywords Explorer:
   - Search your topic idea
   - Record: search volume, keyword difficulty (KD), parent topic
   - Find 3-5 secondary keywords and long-tail variations
   - Check top 10 results — what do they cover? What's missing?

**Target:** KD under 30 for new posts, volume 500+/month

## Step 2: Outline

Create an outline before writing. Structure:

```
# [Title — include primary keyword, under 60 characters]

## Introduction
- Hook: [Open with a relatable scenario, surprising fact, or question]
- Promise: [What will the reader learn/get from this post?]
- Credibility: [Why should they trust GoTravel on this topic?]

## [H2 Section — maps to a secondary keyword or key subtopic]
### [H3 Subsection if needed]

## [H2 Section]

## [H2 Section]

## Practical Tips / What to Know Before You Go
[Actionable takeaways]

## FAQ
[3-5 questions from "People Also Ask" in Google]

## CTA
[Soft pitch to a relevant GoTravel trip]
```

**Rules:**
- Minimum 5 H2 sections
- Every H2 should be scannable — a reader skimming headings should get the gist
- Include primary keyword in H1 and at least one H2
- Front-load the most valuable information (don't bury it)

## Step 3: Write the Draft

Follow the GoTravel Voice Guide (`_context/GoTravel_Voice_Guide.md`):

- **Tone:** Informative + storytelling + relatable
- **POV:** First person plural ("we") or second person ("you")
- **Length:** 1,500-2,500 words depending on topic depth
- **Paragraphs:** Max 3-4 sentences each
- **Subheadings:** Every 200-300 words

**Writing checklist:**
- [ ] Opens with a hook, not a generic intro ("Southeast Asia is a beautiful region...")
- [ ] Includes personal experience or specific anecdotes where possible
- [ ] Uses sensory language for destination descriptions (sights, sounds, smells, tastes)
- [ ] Provides genuinely useful, specific information (not vague "do your research")
- [ ] Naturally includes primary keyword 3-5 times (not stuffed)
- [ ] Includes secondary keywords where natural
- [ ] Has internal links to 2-3 relevant trip pages or other blog posts
- [ ] Has 1-2 external links to authoritative sources
- [ ] Ends with a clear but soft CTA to a relevant GoTravel trip

## Step 4: Optimize with SurferSEO

1. Open SurferSEO → Content Editor
2. Enter the primary keyword
3. Paste your draft
4. Aim for a content score of 70+
5. Adjust based on recommendations:
   - Add missing keywords/terms
   - Adjust word count if significantly off
   - Add more headings or images if suggested
6. Don't sacrifice readability for score — voice > optimization

## Step 5: Create Featured Image

1. Open Canva Pro → Blog Thumbnail template (1200x630)
2. Use a high-quality photo from the relevant destination
3. Add a warm tone overlay (per Brand Style Guide)
4. Add title text in Plus Jakarta Sans Bold, white, with subtle shadow
5. Export as PNG and JPG (JPG for web, PNG for social)
6. Name: `gotravel-blog-{topic-slug}-{YYYY-MM}.jpg`

## Step 6: SEO Metadata

Before publishing, write:

- **Meta title:** Under 60 characters, includes primary keyword, compelling
  - Format: `[Primary Keyword] — GoTravel Adventures` or `[Number] [Topic] | GoTravel`
  - Example: "Best Vietnam Itinerary for 2 Weeks — GoTravel Adventures"
- **Meta description:** 150-155 characters, includes primary keyword, has a value proposition and soft CTA
  - Example: "Plan the perfect 2-week Vietnam trip with our day-by-day itinerary. Covers Hanoi, Sapa, Ha Long Bay, Hoi An & more. See the full route."
- **URL slug:** Short, lowercase, hyphens, primary keyword
  - Example: `/blog/vietnam-itinerary-2-weeks`
- **Alt text for images:** Descriptive, includes keyword where natural

## Step 7: Internal Review

Before publishing:
- [ ] Read the full post aloud — does it sound like GoTravel?
- [ ] Check all links work
- [ ] Check spelling and grammar
- [ ] Confirm images load and have alt text
- [ ] Preview on mobile — is it readable?
- [ ] SurferSEO score is 70+

## Step 8: Publish & Distribute

1. Publish on Shopify blog
2. Submit URL to Google Search Console for indexing
3. Share on social media:
   - Instagram: Story with swipe-up link + feed post later in the week
   - Facebook: Post to page + GoTravel Explorers group
   - LinkedIn: If B2B relevant or thought leadership angle
4. Include in next Saturday's Travel Dispatch newsletter
5. Add to content tracker in Notion (title, URL, keyword, publish date)

## Step 9: Monitor Performance (2 Weeks Later)

1. Check Google Search Console: Is it indexing? What queries is it ranking for?
2. Check GA4: Sessions, time on page, bounce rate
3. Check Ahrefs: Keyword positions
4. If underperforming after 30 days:
   - Update content based on actual search queries
   - Add more internal links from other pages
   - Improve the intro/hook
   - Consider refreshing the title/meta description

---

## Quality Standards
- Never publish a post under 1,200 words
- Never stuff keywords — if it reads awkwardly, rewrite naturally
- Every post must provide standalone value even if the reader never books a trip
- Accuracy matters — double-check visa info, prices, distances, and opening hours
