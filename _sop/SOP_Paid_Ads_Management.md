# SOP: Paid Ads Management

## Purpose
Run, optimize, and report on paid advertising campaigns across Meta (Instagram + Facebook) and Google Ads to drive bookings at a target CPA.

## Frequency
- **Daily:** Quick spend and performance check (5 min)
- **Twice weekly (Mon/Thu):** Optimization pass
- **Monthly:** Full review and strategy adjustment

## Tools Required
- Meta Business Suite / Ads Manager
- Google Ads
- Google Analytics 4
- Google Sheets (performance tracker)

## Budget
- **Meta Ads:** $3,000/month
- **Google Ads:** $1,200/month
- **TikTok Ads:** $300/month (testing)
- **Total:** $4,500/month

## Target Metrics
| Metric | Target |
|---|---|
| Cost per acquisition (booking) | < $35 |
| ROAS (return on ad spend) | > 30x |
| Click-through rate (Meta) | > 1.5% |
| Click-through rate (Google Search) | > 4% |
| Cost per click (Meta) | < $1.50 |
| Cost per click (Google) | < $2.00 |

## Campaign Structure

### Meta Ads (Instagram + Facebook)

**Campaign 1: Prospecting (Top of Funnel)**
- **Objective:** Conversions (trip page views or bookings)
- **Budget:** $60/day
- **Audiences:**
  - Lookalike 1% of past bookers
  - Lookalike 1% of email list
  - Interest targeting: Adventure travel, Southeast Asia, Lonely Planet, Intrepid Travel, backpacking, travel photography
  - Age: 25-45, Locations: US, UK, Canada, Australia
- **Ad sets:** Test 2-3 audience segments per campaign
- **Creatives:** Test 3 variations:
  1. Carousel: 5 trip photos with highlights as captions
  2. Single image: Hero destination photo + benefit headline
  3. Video/Reel: 15-30 second trip highlight montage
- **Copy formula:**
  - Line 1: Hook — destination + emotion or curiosity
  - Line 2-3: Key benefits (small groups, local guides, all-inclusive)
  - Line 4: Social proof (rating, number of trips run)
  - Line 5: CTA with price anchor ("From $1,649 all-inclusive → Book now")

**Campaign 2: Retargeting (Middle/Bottom of Funnel)**
- **Objective:** Conversions (bookings)
- **Budget:** $30/day
- **Audiences:**
  - Website visitors (last 30 days) who didn't book
  - Trip page viewers (last 14 days)
  - Email subscribers who clicked but didn't book
  - Instagram/Facebook engagers (last 30 days)
- **Creatives:**
  - Testimonial-style: Traveler photo + review quote
  - Urgency: "Only X spots left on [Trip Name]"
  - Objection-handling: "Yes, it's all-inclusive. Here's everything that's covered."
- **Frequency cap:** Max 3 impressions per person per week (avoid ad fatigue)

**Campaign 3: Seasonal/Promo (As Needed)**
- **Objective:** Conversions
- **Budget:** Variable (pull from prospecting budget)
- **When:** New trip launches, seasonal promotions, last-minute departure deals
- **Duration:** 1-2 weeks max
- **Audiences:** Warm audiences first, then expand to prospecting

### Google Ads

**Campaign 1: Branded Search**
- **Keywords:** "GoTravel Adventures", "GoTravel tours", "GoTravel reviews"
- **Budget:** $5/day (defensive — protect brand name)
- **Bid strategy:** Target impression share (top of page)

**Campaign 2: Non-Branded Search (High Intent)**
- **Keywords:**
  - "book Vietnam tour"
  - "Southeast Asia group tour"
  - "Thailand small group travel"
  - "Cambodia tour package"
  - "best Vietnam itinerary"
- **Negative keywords:** "free", "DIY", "volunteer", "jobs", "cheap flights"
- **Budget:** $35/day
- **Bid strategy:** Maximize conversions with target CPA ($35)
- **Ad format:** Responsive Search Ads
  - Headlines (15 variations): Mix of destination, benefit, social proof, pricing
  - Descriptions (4 variations): Value prop, what's included, CTA
  - Sitelink extensions: "See All Trips", "Vietnam Explorer", "About Us", "Reviews"

### TikTok Ads (Testing Phase)

- **Campaign type:** Spark Ads (boosting top organic content)
- **Budget:** $10/day
- **Audience:** 25-40, travel interest, US/UK/AU
- **Goal:** Awareness + profile visits (not direct conversions yet)
- **Content:** Only boost Reels/TikToks that already have 2x average organic views

## Daily Check (5 Minutes)

1. Open Meta Ads Manager → check:
   - Total spend today vs. daily budget (is anything overspending?)
   - Any campaign errors or disapproved ads?
   - Any CPA spikes above $60? (flag for investigation)
2. Open Google Ads → check:
   - Spend on track?
   - Any disapproved ads?
   - Search terms report: any irrelevant queries burning budget?
3. No action needed if metrics are within normal range

---

## Twice-Weekly Optimization (Monday & Thursday)

### Step 1: Review Performance by Ad Set / Ad Group
In Meta Ads Manager:
1. Sort ad sets by CPA (highest first)
2. **Kill** any ad set with CPA > $50 AND 1,000+ impressions (enough data to judge)
3. **Reduce budget** on ad sets with CPA $35-50
4. **Increase budget 20%** on ad sets with CPA < $25 (scale winners gradually)
5. Check frequency — if any ad set is above 3.0, refresh creative or pause

In Google Ads:
1. Check Search Terms report — add irrelevant queries as negative keywords
2. Check keyword performance — pause any keyword with $50+ spend and 0 conversions
3. Check ad copy performance — pause lowest-performing responsive ad variations

### Step 2: Review Creative Performance
1. Sort ads by CTR and CPA
2. Identify best and worst creative per campaign
3. Pause any ad with CTR < 0.8% after 2,000+ impressions
4. Note what's working (format, image style, copy angle) for next creative batch

### Step 3: Check Landing Pages
1. Open GA4 → Landing Pages report
2. Check trip page conversion rates — is any page significantly underperforming?
3. If a page has high traffic but low conversion (< 1.5%), check Hotjar recordings for issues

### Step 4: Budget Reallocation
- If one campaign is crushing it and another is underperforming, shift budget
- Never shift more than 20% of a campaign's budget at once
- Document any changes in the performance tracker

1. Which audience segments have the lowest CPA?

## Creative Refresh Schedule

Ad fatigue is real. Refresh creatives on this cadence:
- **Prospecting ads:** New creatives every 2-3 weeks
- **Retargeting ads:** New creatives every 3-4 weeks
- **Google Search ads:** Update headlines/descriptions monthly

**Creative production batch:**
- Every 2 weeks, produce:
  - 2 new static images
  - 1 new carousel
  - 1 new video/Reel (15-30s)
- Use Canva for static, native phone editing or CapCut for video

## Troubleshooting

| Problem | Likely Cause | Fix |
|---|---|---|
| CPA suddenly spiked | Ad fatigue, audience saturation, or seasonal dip | Refresh creative, test new audience, check if competitor entered the auction |
| CTR dropped | Creative fatigue or poor audience match | Swap creatives, tighten targeting |
| High clicks but no conversions | Landing page issue | Check page speed, mobile UX, booking flow in Hotjar |
| Ad disapproved | Policy violation (often image text or claims) | Review Meta/Google policies, edit and resubmit |
| Budget not spending | Audience too narrow or bid too low | Broaden targeting or switch to automatic bidding |
