# SOP: Social Media Weekly Workflow

## Purpose
Maintain a consistent, high-quality social media presence across all platforms with a repeatable weekly workflow.

## Frequency
Weekly cycle: Plan on Monday, create Tuesday-Wednesday, schedule Thursday, engage daily.

## Tools Required
- Later (scheduling & analytics)
- Canva Pro (design)
- Instagram / TikTok / Facebook (native posting for Reels/Stories)
- Notion (content calendar)
- Google Analytics 4 (traffic tracking)

---

## Monday: Review & Plan

### Step 1: Review Last Week's Performance
1. Open Later → Analytics for each platform
2. Record in Notion content tracker:
   - Top performing post (by engagement rate)
   - Worst performing post
   - Follower growth (net)
   - Total reach and impressions
   - Profile visits and link clicks
3. Note patterns: What topic/format/time worked best?

### Step 2: Plan This Week's Content
Refer to the content calendar in Notion. If slots are empty, fill them using this mix:

**Instagram (5 posts/week):**
| Day | Format | Content Type |
|---|---|---|
| Monday | Carousel or Static | Educational — travel tip, packing list, "X things to know before visiting [country]" |
| Tuesday | Reel | Trip highlight — "day in the life" or destination showcase |
| Wednesday | Static or Carousel | Traveler story / UGC repost (with permission) |
| Friday | Reel | Trending format — use trending audio + travel angle |
| Saturday | Carousel | Trip promotion — itinerary breakdown or "what's included" |

**Instagram Stories (daily):**
- Mix of: behind-the-scenes, polls, Q&A, reshared posts, trip countdown stickers, travel quotes

**TikTok (4 posts/week):**
- Repurpose Instagram Reels + 1-2 TikTok-native trends

**Facebook (3 posts/week):**
- Repurpose top Instagram posts + 1 post in GoTravel Explorers group

### Step 3: Assign Captions & Assets
For each planned post, note in the content calendar:
- Topic/angle
- Caption draft (or assign to write)
- Visual needed (photo, video, graphic)
- Hashtags (pull from hashtag bank — see below)
- CTA type (soft/medium/strong)

---

## Tuesday-Wednesday: Create Content

### Step 4: Write Captions
Follow `_context/GoTravel_Voice_Guide.md`. For each caption:

**Structure:**
1. **Hook (line 1):** The most important line — this is all people see before "more." Make it stop the scroll.
   - Good: "I almost didn't go to Luang Prabang. Biggest mistake I nearly made."
   - Bad: "Check out this amazing photo from Laos!"
2. **Body (2-5 lines):** Story, tip, or insight. Keep paragraphs to 1-2 sentences. Use line breaks.
3. **CTA (last line):** Ask a question, invite a comment, or direct to link in bio.
   - "Have you been? Drop your favorite memory below."
   - "Full itinerary → link in bio"

**Caption rules:**
- Write 3 variations of the hook — pick the most scroll-stopping one
- No hashtags in the caption body — put them in the first comment
- Use emojis sparingly (max 2-3 per caption, never to replace words)
- Tag locations and relevant accounts
- Keep under 150 words for feed posts (longer for carousels)

### Step 5: Create Visuals
For photos:
1. Select from the photo library or request from operations/travelers
2. Edit in Canva or Lightroom: warm tone, consistent with Brand Style Guide
3. Crop to platform specs (1080x1080 feed, 1080x1920 Stories/Reels)

For Reels/TikTok:
1. Edit video clips to 15-30 seconds (60 max for storytelling Reels)
2. Add captions/subtitles (80% of people watch without sound)
3. Use trending audio where relevant — check Reels trending page
4. Add GoTravel logo watermark in corner

For carousels:
1. Use Canva carousel template (GoTravel branded)
2. First slide = hook (bold text, eye-catching)
3. Last slide = CTA ("Save this for your trip" or "Follow for more")
4. Keep text concise — max 30 words per slide
5. Export as individual images (not PDF)

### Step 6: Prepare Hashtag Sets
Maintain 3-4 hashtag sets in Notion and rotate:

**Set A — General travel:**
#SmallGroupTravel #SoutheastAsiaTravel #TravelAsia #AdventureTravel #GroupTourAsia #AsiaTrip #TravelWithLocals #ResponsibleTravel #GoTravelAdventures

**Set B — Destination specific (example: Vietnam):**
#VietnamTravel #ExploreVietnam #HanoiStreetFood #HaLongBay #SapaRiceTerrace #HoiAn #VietnamItinerary #GoTravelAdventures

**Set C — Engagement/community:**
#TravelCommunity #WanderMore #TravelInspiration #TravelTips #NextTrip #TravelGoals #GroupTravelLife #GoTravelAdventures

**Rules:**
- Max 15 hashtags per Instagram post (first comment, not caption)
- Always include #GoTravelAdventures and #SmallGroupTravel
- Rotate sets — don't use the same set twice in a row
- No banned or spam hashtags (#FollowForFollow, #Like4Like)
- TikTok: 3-5 hashtags max, in the caption

---

## Thursday: Schedule

### Step 7: Schedule Posts in Later
1. Upload all visuals and captions to Later
2. Set publish times based on best engagement windows:
   - Instagram: 7-9am and 6-8pm (audience timezone — check analytics for exact peak)
   - TikTok: 10am-12pm and 7-9pm
   - Facebook: 9-11am
3. Double-check:
   - [ ] Caption reads well (no typos, tone is right)
   - [ ] First image is the strongest (for carousels)
   - [ ] Location tag added
   - [ ] Accounts tagged where relevant
   - [ ] Hashtag comment prepared
4. Schedule Stories manually (Later has limited Stories scheduling — prepare them in Canva and set reminders)

### Step 8: Queue Reels & TikToks
- Reels and TikToks often perform better posted natively
- Save the edited video to phone
- Set a reminder to post at the scheduled time
- Post natively with caption, audio, and hashtags

---

## Daily: Engage

### Step 9: Community Management (15-20 min/day)
1. **Reply to all comments** on posts within 12 hours
   - Reply with personality — not just "Thanks!" but actual conversation
   - Ask follow-up questions to boost comment count
   - Heart/like every comment at minimum
2. **Reply to all DMs** within 24 hours
   - Booking questions → direct to trip page or email team
   - General questions → answer helpfully, mention relevant trip if natural
   - Spam → delete and block
3. **Engage with others** (10 min/day)
   - Comment on 5-10 posts from travel creators, past travelers, and destination accounts
   - Reshare tagged content to Stories (with thank you)
   - Engage in relevant travel hashtag feeds

### Step 10: Monitor for UGC
- Check tags and mentions daily
- Screenshot/save any great user-generated content
- DM the creator to ask permission to repost
- Save approved UGC to the content library in Notion

---

## Monthly: Audit & Refresh

### Step 11: Monthly Social Audit (First Monday of Month)
1. Export analytics from Later for the full month
2. Identify:
   - Top 5 posts by engagement
   - Top 3 posts by reach
   - Content type breakdown (which format performs best?)
   - Best posting times (has the window shifted?)
   - Follower demographics (any changes?)
3. Update the content mix if needed (e.g., Reels crushing static → shift ratio)
4. Refresh hashtag sets based on performance
5. Archive the month's data in `reports/social/social-audit-YYYY-MM.md`
